About Love Touches

Relationships take work. They need attention, proding, improving. It is easy to fall into a rut and to take your relationship for granted rather than pursing the best possible relationship.

Love Touches exists to provide you with a simple scheduled set of reminders, giving you a diversity of ideas to help you revitalize or enhance your relationship.
